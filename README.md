## What Is Dealfront, Actually?

Dealfront is a go-to-market platform born out of the 2022 merger between two well-known names in the B2B space: **Echobot** (a German sales intelligence platform founded in 2011) and **Leadfeeder** (a Finnish website visitor identification tool founded in 2012). The merger was backed by a ‚Ç¨180M investment from Great Hill Partners, and the company officially launched as Dealfront in April 2023.

Since then, they've kept building. In 2024, they pulled in an additional ‚Ç¨30M from AshGrove Capital to fuel European expansion. Today, Dealfront has 250+ employees across 30+ countries, serves over 30,000 customer companies, and hosts a database covering 60+ million companies and 400+ million contacts.

The platform is headquartered in Karlsruhe, Germany, with a major office in Helsinki ‚Äî and it's very much built with European markets in mind. GDPR compliance isn't an afterthought here; it's baked into the foundation. They host servers in Europe, source data from public registers, and actually show you *where* each data point came from. That kind of transparency is genuinely rare in this space.

## How It Works (The Short Version)

You install a lightweight tracking code on your website. Dealfront's system then uses IP matching, machine learning, and its own expansive databases to figure out which *companies* those anonymous visitors work for ‚Äî even if those employees are working remotely from home.

Once a company is identified, Dealfront enriches it with firmographic data (industry, size, location, revenue), pulls up relevant contacts like decision-makers, and surfaces behavioral signals like which pages were visited, how long they stayed, and whether they hit high-intent pages like pricing or demo requests.

The result? Anonymous website traffic becomes a list of real, actionable company leads ‚Äî automatically pushed into your CRM.

## The Platform Modules ‚Äî What You're Actually Getting

Dealfront isn't one monolithic tool. It's built as a set of focused modules that work together (or standalone, depending on what you need):

**Leadfeeder (Web Visitors)** ‚Äî This is the flagship. It identifies companies visiting your website in real time, scores them by intent, and syncs them straight to your CRM. It's the module with the most transparent, self-serve pricing.

**Target** ‚Äî A B2B prospecting tool for building lists of companies that match your Ideal Customer Profile. Uses 100+ filters across firmographics, tech stacks, and behavior signals. Great for outbound teams that need a pipeline of new-name prospects.

**Connect** ‚Äî Deep-dive company and contact profiles. Includes a Chrome browser extension so sales reps can research accounts on the fly without leaving their workflow.

**Datacare** ‚Äî Keeps your existing CRM data clean, enriched, and accurate. If your CRM has been accumulating stale records for years, this is the module that fixes that.

**Promote** ‚Äî IP-based B2B display advertising. Lets you retarget specific companies with ads, which works especially well when combined with Leadfeeder's visitor data.

**Dealfront AI** (launched May 2025) ‚Äî A newer addition that adds AI Company Insights (ask natural language questions about any prospect), AI Contacts (surfaces key decision-maker info automatically), and AI Activity Summaries (plain-language breakdowns of visitor behavior).

## Who Should Actually Use This?

Dealfront hits the sweet spot for **B2B companies with steady website traffic who sell into European markets**. If you've got visitors coming to your site and you're not capturing who those companies are, this is a legitimate solution.

It's especially strong for teams that already use CRM tools like Salesforce, HubSpot, or Pipedrive ‚Äî the integrations are native and two-way, meaning data flows without manual work.

If you're a small startup with very low traffic (say, under 50 unique company visitors per month), the entry-level paid tier at $99/month will cover you, but you might want to start on the free plan first to see what kind of volume you're actually getting.

For larger enterprise teams with high traffic volumes and multiple sales reps, the higher tiers deliver real value ‚Äî especially when combined with the Sales Intelligence modules for outbound prospecting and the AI features for faster qualification.
